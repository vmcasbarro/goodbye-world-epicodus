Goodbye World

A program to say farewell. :/

This page is an attempt to say goodbye to everyone on Earth using our various human languages. We're interested to see how long our list can become.

We'll start with languages that the Epicodus staff could offer without using Google.

  • English: Goodbye, world!
  • Spanish: Adios, mundo!
  • Italian: Ciao, mundo!
  • Pig Latin: oodbye-gay, orld-way!
